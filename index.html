If this is your first time on the page,
then you need to calibrate it.
Click around the page around 9 ~ 15 times, preferably on the corners,
the spaces between the corners, and the center

If you have already calibrated the website before,
then you can have a little cursor with your eyes.
If it is not working for you, or it is too inaccurate, you can still use the mouse.

In order to increase and decrease the number,
move your eyes to the edges.
When you move your eyes to the left and hold it for one second,
then it will decrease one and vice versa.
